APICULTURE FEEDING

Feeding keeps bee colonies healthy and productive when nature falls short, supporting hive strength, brood growth, and steady honey flow.

🍯 Bee Feeding Essentials

Feeding isn’t just support — it’s survival. Keep your hive healthy during lean seasons.

Feeding honeybees is crucial during seasons when natural food sources are low. A well-fed colony remains active, healthy, and productive year-round. Choosing the right feed and method ensures bees receive essential nutrition.

🥣 Feeding Options for Bees

🍬 Sugar Syrup

Used in 1:1 or 2:1 ratio based on season. Provides instant energy during low nectar periods.

🥩 Protein Patties

Great for brood development. Use during early spring or pollen shortage.

🌾 Pollen Substitutes

Supplement when real pollen is unavailable. Helps with colony strength.

💧 Water Supply

Clean water should always be available near the hive to maintain hydration.

💡 Helpful Feeding Tips

✅ Use fresh sugar syrup — never fermented.
✅ Clean feeding tools and trays after each use.
✅ Don’t overfeed. Observe hive behavior.
✅ Place feeders away from ants or wasp paths.
✅ Avoid feeding during natural nectar flow.
