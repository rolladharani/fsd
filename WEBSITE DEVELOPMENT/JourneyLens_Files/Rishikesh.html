About Rishikesh:

Rishikesh, the "Yoga Capital of the World," is a serene town nestled in the Himalayas.
It is famous for its spiritual retreats, adventure sports, and breathtaking landscapes.
Visitors can experience yoga, meditation, river rafting, and explore ancient temples.

Best Places to Visit in Rishikesh

  • Lakshman Jhula - A historic suspension bridge over the Ganges.
  • Triveni Ghat - Famous for the evening Ganga Aarti.
  • Ram Jhula - Another iconic bridge connecting temples and ashrams.
  • The Beatles Ashram - A spiritual retreat visited by The Beatles.
  • Neelkanth Mahadev Temple - A revered Shiva temple.
  • Rajaji National Park - A wildlife sanctuary with elephants and tigers.
  • Shivpuri - A hub for river rafting and camping.

Local Uttarakhand Cuisines:

  • Aloo Puri - Spicy potato curry with deep-fried bread.
  • Chole Bhature - A North Indian favorite.
  • Kafuli - A spinach-based dish unique to Uttarakhand.
  • Thukpa - A Tibetan noodle soup.
  • Bal Mithai - A sweet made from roasted khoya.
  • Jhangora Kheer - A millet-based dessert.
  • Masala Chai - Spiced tea, perfect for the chilly weather.

Best Seasons to Visit Rishikesh:

  • Spring (March - May) - Ideal for yoga retreats and sightseeing.
  • Monsoon (July - September) - Lush greenery but limited adventure activities.
  • Winter (October - February) - Perfect for peaceful meditation and rafting.
