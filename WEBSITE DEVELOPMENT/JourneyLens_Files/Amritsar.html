About Amritsar:

Amritsar, the spiritual and cultural heart of Punjab, is home to the iconic Golden Temple.
The city is steeped in history, with landmarks like Jallianwala Bagh and Wagah Border.
Visitors can explore vibrant bazaars, taste authentic Punjabi cuisine, and experience the rich Sikh heritage.

Best Places to Visit in Amritsar

  • Golden Temple - The holiest Sikh shrine, known for its stunning architecture.
  • Jallianwala Bagh - A historic site commemorating the 1919 massacre.
  • Wagah Border - Famous for the daily flag-lowering ceremony.
  • Partition Museum - Showcasing stories from India's partition era.
  • Durgiana Temple - A Hindu temple resembling the Golden Temple.
  • Gobindgarh Fort - A historic fort with cultural performances.
  • Akal Takht - One of the five seats of Sikh authority.

Local Punjabi Cuisines:

  • Amritsari Kulcha - Stuffed bread served with spicy chole.
  • Makki di Roti & Sarson da Saag - A classic Punjabi winter dish.
  • Butter Chicken - Creamy and flavorful chicken curry.
  • Langar Ka Prasad - Free meal served at the Golden Temple.
  • Lassi - A refreshing yogurt-based drink.
  • Paneer Tikka - Grilled cottage cheese with spices.
  • Chole Bhature - Spicy chickpeas with fluffy fried bread.

Best Seasons to Visit Amritsar:

  • Winter (October - March) - Pleasant weather for sightseeing.
  • Spring (April - June) - Ideal for cultural festivals.
  • Monsoon (July - September) - Lush greenery but occasional rain.
