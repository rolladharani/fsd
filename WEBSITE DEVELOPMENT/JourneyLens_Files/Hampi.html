About Hampi:

Hampi, a UNESCO World Heritage Site, is an ancient city in Karnataka.
It is famous for its stunning ruins, temples, and historical significance.
Visitors can explore stone-carved monuments, royal enclosures, and vibrant markets.

Best Places to Visit in Hampi

  • Virupaksha Temple - A historic temple dedicated to Lord Shiva.
  • Vittala Temple - Famous for its musical pillars and stone chariot.
  • Hampi Bazaar - A lively market with local crafts.
  • Lotus Mahal - A beautiful Indo-Islamic architectural marvel.
  • Matanga Hill - Offers breathtaking sunrise views.

Local Hampi Cuisines:

  • Bisi Bele Bath - A spicy rice and lentil dish.
  • Ragi Mudde - Finger millet balls served with curry.
  • Mysore Pak - A delicious South Indian sweet.
  • Filter Coffee - Authentic South Indian coffee.

Best Seasons to Visit Hampi:

  • Winter (October - March) - Best for exploring ruins.
  • Monsoon (July - September) - Lush greenery but occasional rain.
