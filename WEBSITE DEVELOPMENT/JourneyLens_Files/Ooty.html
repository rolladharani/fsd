About Ooty:

Ooty, the "Queen of Hill Stations," is a picturesque retreat in Tamil Nadu.
It is known for its lush greenery, tea gardens, and colonial charm.
Visitors can enjoy scenic train rides, boating, and trekking.

Best Places to Visit in Ooty

  • Ooty Lake - A serene lake with boating facilities.
  • Botanical Gardens - Home to exotic plants and flowers.
  • Doddabetta Peak - The highest peak in the Nilgiris.
  • Rose Garden - A beautiful garden with thousands of rose varieties.
  • Nilgiri Mountain Railway - A UNESCO heritage toy train ride.

Local Ooty Cuisines:

  • Ooty Varkey - A crispy baked snack.
  • Tea & Biscuits - Famous Nilgiri tea with local biscuits.
  • Avial - A mixed vegetable dish with coconut.
  • Chicken Chettinad - A spicy South Indian chicken curry.

Best Seasons to Visit Ooty:

  • Summer (March - June) - Ideal for sightseeing.
  • Winter (October - February) - Perfect for cozy stays.
