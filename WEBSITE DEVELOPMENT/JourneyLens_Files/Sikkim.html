About Sikkim:

Sikkim, nestled in the Himalayas, is a paradise of natural beauty and rich culture.
It is known for its monasteries, breathtaking landscapes, and adventure activities.
Visitors can explore high-altitude lakes, trek through scenic valleys, and experience vibrant festivals.

Best Places to Visit in Sikkim

  • Gangtok - The capital city with monasteries and MG Marg.
  • Nathula Pass - A high-altitude mountain pass on the Indo-China border.
  • Tsomgo Lake - A stunning glacial lake surrounded by mountains.
  • Yumthang Valley - Known as the "Valley of Flowers."
  • Pelling - Offers breathtaking views of Kanchenjunga.

Local Sikkim Cuisines:

  • Momos - A staple Tibetan dumpling dish.
  • Phagshapa - Pork cooked with radish and dried chilies.
  • Sel Roti - A traditional rice-based snack.
  • Gundruk - Fermented leafy greens.

Best Seasons to Visit Sikkim:

  • Spring (March - May) - Best for trekking and sightseeing.
  • Autumn (September - November) - Ideal for clear mountain views.
  • Winter (December - February) - Perfect for snow lovers.
