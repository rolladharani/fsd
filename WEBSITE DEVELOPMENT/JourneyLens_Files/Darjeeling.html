About Darjeeling:

Darjeeling, the "Queen of the Himalayas," is a breathtaking hill station in West Bengal.
It is famous for its tea plantations, stunning mountain views, and colonial charm.
Visitors can enjoy scenic train rides, explore monasteries, and witness the sunrise at Tiger Hill.

Best Places to Visit in Darjeeling

  • Tiger Hill - Famous for breathtaking sunrise views over Kanchenjunga.
  • Darjeeling Himalayan Railway - A UNESCO World Heritage toy train ride.
  • Batasia Loop - A scenic railway loop with panoramic views.
  • Padmaja Naidu Himalayan Zoological Park - Home to red pandas and snow leopards.
  • Peace Pagoda - A serene Buddhist stupa offering tranquility.

Local Darjeeling Cuisines:

  • Momos - Steamed dumplings filled with meat or vegetables.
  • Thukpa - A Tibetan noodle soup.
  • Darjeeling Tea - World-famous aromatic tea.
  • Shaphalay - Tibetan bread stuffed with meat.

Best Seasons to Visit Darjeeling:

  • Spring (March - May) - Ideal for sightseeing and tea gardens.
  • Autumn (September - November) - Pleasant weather with clear mountain views.
  • Winter (December - February) - Perfect for cozy stays and misty landscapes.
