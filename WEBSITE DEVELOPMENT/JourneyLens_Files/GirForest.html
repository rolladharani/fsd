About Gir Forest:

Gir Forest, the last refuge of the Asiatic lions, is a wildlife sanctuary in Gujarat.
Visitors can embark on thrilling safaris, explore diverse flora and fauna, and witness majestic lions in their natural habitat.

Best Places to Visit in Gir Forest

  • Gir National Park - Home to the Asiatic lions.
  • Devalia Safari Park - A fenced area for easy wildlife spotting.
  • Kankai Temple - A serene temple inside the forest.
  • Kamleshwar Dam - A scenic spot with crocodiles and birds.
  • Somnath Temple - A historic temple near Gir.

Local Gujarati Cuisines:

  • Khandvi - A soft, rolled snack made from gram flour.
  • Thepla - A spiced flatbread.
  • Undhiyu - A mixed vegetable dish cooked with spices.
  • Fafda & Jalebi - A popular breakfast combination.

Best Seasons to Visit Gir Forest:

  • Winter (November - February) - Best for wildlife safaris.
  • Summer (March - May) - Good for lion sightings but hot weather.
