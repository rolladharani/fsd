About Shillong:

Shillong, the "Scotland of the East," is a charming hill station in Meghalaya.
It is known for its lush greenery, waterfalls, and vibrant culture.
Visitors can explore scenic lakes, trek through canyons, and experience local Khasi traditions.

Best Places to Visit in Shillong

  • Umiam Lake - A picturesque reservoir surrounded by hills.
  • Elephanst Falls - A stunning multi-tiered waterfall.
  • Laitlum Canyons - Offers breathtaking panoramic views.
  • Don Bosco Museum - Showcasing Northeast India's rich heritage.
  • Police Bazar - A bustling market for shopping and street food.

Local Shillong Cuisines:

  • Jadoh - A Khasi-style rice and meat dish.
  • Dohneiiong - Pork cooked with black sesame seeds.
  • Tungrymbai - Fermented soybean dish.
  • Pumaloi - Steamed rice cake.

Best Seasons to Visit Shillong:

  • Spring (March - May) - Ideal for sightseeing and trekking.
  • Autumn (September - November) - Pleasant weather with clear skies.
  • Winter (December - February) - Perfect for cozy stays.
