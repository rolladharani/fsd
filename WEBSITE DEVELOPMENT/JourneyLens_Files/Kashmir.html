About Kashmir:

Kashmir, often called "Paradise on Earth," is a breathtaking travel destination in North India.
It boasts stunning landscapes, serene lakes, lush valleys, and snow-capped mountains. Visitors can
enjoy activities like Shikara rides on Dal Lake, trekking in the Himalayas, and exploring Mughal gardens.
Popular spots include Gulmarg, Pahalgam, Sonmarg, and Srinagar. The region is also known for its rich culture,
warm hospitality, and delicious Kashmiri cuisine.

Best Places to Visit in Kashmir

  • Srinagar - Famous for Dal Lake, Shikara rides, and Mughal Gardens.
  • Gulmarg - A paradise for skiing, snowboarding, and gondola rides.
  • Pahalgam - Known for lush valleys, river rafting, and trekking.
  • Leh-Ladakh - Stunning mountain landscapes, monasteries, and adventure sports.
  • Betaab Valley - A picturesque valley with green meadows and crystal-clear streams.
  • Tulip Garden - Asia's largest tulip garden, best visited in spring.
  • Shankaracharya Temple -A historic temple with panoramic views of Srinagar

Local Kashmiri Cuisines:

  • Rogan Josh - A flavorful lamb curry with aromatic spices.
  • Modur Pulav - Sweet Kashmiri rice cooked with saffron and dry fruits.
  • Yakhni - A yogurt-based lamb curry with mild spices.
  • Dum Aloo - Spicy potato dish cooked with yogurt and Kashmiri spices.
  • Goshtaba - Minced meatballs cooked in a creamy yogurt gravy.
  • Kahwa - Traditional Kashmiri tea infused with saffron and almonds.
  • Shab Deg - Slow-cooked meat dish with turnips and spices.
  • Lyader Tschaman - Cottage cheese cooked in a rich turmeric-based gravy.

Best Seasons to Visit Kashmir:

  • Spring (March - May) - Best for tulip gardens, blossoming flowers, and pleasant weather.
  • Summer (June - August) - Ideal for trekking, houseboat stays, and exploring valleys.
  • Autumn (September - November) - Witness golden Chinar trees, apple orchards, and cool breezes.
  • Winter (December - February) - Perfect for snow activities, skiing in Gulmarg, and frozen lakes.
