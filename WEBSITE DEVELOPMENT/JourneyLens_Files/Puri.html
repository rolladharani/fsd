About Puri:

Puri, a sacred coastal city in Odisha, is famous for the Jagannath Temple and pristine beaches.
It is a major pilgrimage site and offers a blend of spirituality, history, and scenic beauty.
Visitors can explore ancient temples, relax on golden beaches, and witness the grand Rath Yatra festival.

Best Places to Visit in Puri

  • Jagannath Temple - A revered Hindu temple dedicated to Lord Jagannath.
  • Puri Beach - A serene beach perfect for relaxation.
  • Konark Sun Temple - A UNESCO World Heritage Site.
  • Chilika Lake - A stunning lagoon with migratory birds.
  • Raghurajpur Artist Village - Famous for traditional Pattachitra paintings.

Local Puri Cuisines:

  • Dalma - A lentil and vegetable dish.
  • Chhena Poda - A caramelized cheese dessert.
  • Pakhala Bhata - Fermented rice dish.
  • Rasagola - A famous sweet from Odisha.

Best Seasons to Visit Puri:

  • Winter (October - March) - Best for sightseeing and temple visits.
  • Monsoon (July - September) - Lush greenery but occasional rain.
